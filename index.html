80/20

Mark:
Google’s 80 20 model
Several years ago, Google implemented a new way of working.
1 day a week, it’s employees could work on anything they wanted. There was just 1 catch. They had to stay in the office.
The idea was to allow its group to innovate.
This is a little different for us:
80 percent core business
Work cannot stop
We have a lot to do
These things need to get done
Need to be efficient
20 percent exploration and innovation
There’s a lot to learn.
Are there trainings or skills you need to develop?
Are there interinstitutional projects you wish you could work on, but don’t have the time.
Will these help the library, libraries, or you personally grow.
Are there interesting projects we could tackle together?
